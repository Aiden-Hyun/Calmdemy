Delete Your Calmdemy Account

Calmdemy gives you full control over your account and data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly from the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Calmdemy app on your device
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom-right)
  3. Tap Account & Security
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

Once confirmed, your account and associated data are permanently deleted immediately. This action cannot be undone.

What is deleted

  • Your user profile and authentication credentials
  • Your meditation history, session logs, and streaks
  • Your favorites and downloaded content references
  • Your preferences and settings

What is retained

  • Payment and subscription records — retained for up to 7 years as required by tax and financial regulations. These are stored by Apple/Google (not by Calmdemy) and are anonymized from your user profile.
  • Anonymized usage analytics — aggregated data (e.g., total minutes meditated across all users) that cannot be traced back to you.
