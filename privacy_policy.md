## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information you provide

- **Account information:** email address and (optionally) your name.
- **Authentication identifiers:** if you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive the authentication token and basic profile attributes that you authorize the provider to share (typically a unique identifier, email, and name). We do not receive your Google or Apple password.
- **Preferences and settings:** notification preferences, language, reminder times, content preferences, and any personalization choices you make.
- **Support communications:** if you contact us, we retain the contents of those communications and any metadata (e.g., email address, timestamp) for the period described in §6.

### 2.2 Information we collect automatically

- **Meditation and listening activity:** sessions started and completed, duration, frequency, content played, listening history, streaks, and aggregated progress statistics.
- **Device and technical data:** device type and model, operating system and version, app version, device language, time zone, and crash and diagnostic logs.
- **Approximate location:** we derive an approximate country or region from your IP address. **We do not collect precise GPS location.**
- **Local storage:** the Calmdemy app uses local storage on your device to persist authentication tokens and preferences. The Service does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking SDKs.

### 2.3 Information from third parties

- **Authentication providers (Google, Apple):** if you choose to sign in with a third-party identity provider, we receive the information described in §2.1. Your use of those providers is governed by their own privacy policies.
- **Infrastructure providers:** see §4.1 for the current list of sub-processors.

### 2.4 Information we do **not** collect

- We do not collect precise geolocation.
- We do not collect biometric identifiers, voice recordings, or microphone audio.
- We do not access your contacts, calendars, or photos.
- We do not request advertising identifiers (IDFA / AAID).
- We do not ask you to disclose medical conditions, diagnoses, or mental-health status.

## 7. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- **Encryption in transit** using TLS 1.2 or higher for all traffic between the app and our backend.
- **Encryption at rest** for databases and storage, as provided by Google Firebase / Google Cloud.
- **Access controls:** administrative access to production systems is restricted, requires multi-factor authentication, and is logged.
- **Authentication:** user authentication is delegated to Firebase Authentication and (optionally) Google or Apple Sign-In; Calmdemy does not store user passwords.
- **Monitoring:** we monitor for anomalous access patterns and apply security patches to dependencies on a regular cadence.
- **Incident response:** in the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and the relevant supervisory authority in accordance with GDPR Art. 33–34 (generally within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach).

No security program is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at **aiden.hyun.business@gmail.com**.

To exercise these rights, contact **aiden.hyun.business@gmail.com**. We will respond within **one month**, extendable by up to two additional months for complex or numerous requests (Art. 12(3)), and we will inform you of any extension within the first month.

### 10.4 Health and wellness data
